Privacy Policy

Poolwatch Log is an offline-first app. All of your data — chlorine (ppm), ph, date, filter cleaned — is stored locally on your device in the app's Application Support directory. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There is no analytics, no tracking, and no account required.

The only network communication the app performs is Apple's own StoreKit purchase and subscription verification, which is handled entirely by Apple and never touches our servers because we don't have any.

If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it.
